First CAS experience



When I went to go visit my cousins, it happened to be one of the younger one's birthday.
So I decided to make her a poster based on a TV show she really likes called Gravity Falls.
The first thing I did was draw up different sketches for ideas I had. When I finally settled
on using the logo as the base design I got a reference photo from online and began creating
an outline. Once I completed the sketch for the Title I used the same process to sketch out the
charater seen on the bottom right. After all of that I used a teabag to get the burnt edges.
I really enjoyed doing this project as paint is not something I normally use in my art.
